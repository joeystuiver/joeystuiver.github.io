Hailing from Maastricht in the Netherlands, Otawanta has been immersed in house music since childhood. Inspired by legends like St. Germain and Todd Terry, Otawanta developed an early appreciation for the genre. Over the course of life, Otawanta picked up an appreciation for Tech-house and Techno. With a strong foundation in traditional house, Otawanta has carved out a unique niche in the modern scene, blending groovy, jazzy Tech-house house with a love for the classics. Recently, Otawanta has begun experimenting with the vibrant sounds of Breakbeat and UK Garage, adding another dynamic layer to the repertoire. Passionate and innovative, Otawanta is redefining the dance music landscape, one track at a time.
